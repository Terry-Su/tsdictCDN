She never was on time!’• Never usually comes after the auxiliary: I have never liked sports.• You sometimes use never before an auxiliary to emphasize that two statements are connected: I don’t enjoy football. I never have liked sports.• In writing, you can use never at the beginning of a sentence before an auxiliary to emphasize something: Never had she been so confused.
