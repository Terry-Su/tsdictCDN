You usually say: the River Thames, the River Seine, the River Rhone, the River Nile, the River Ganges.• In the US and outside Europe, the name usually comes before the word ‘river’. You usually say: the Yangtze River, the Amazon River, the Congo River, the Hudson River, the Mississippi River, the Colorado River.
