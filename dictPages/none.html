You say: We got 3 points and they got none. ✗Don’t say: We got 3 points and they didn’t get none.• You can say not ... any instead of none: We got 3 points and they didn’t get any.Singular or plural verb?• You use none of before a plural noun or pronoun and a singular verb: None of us is able to escape the consequences of our actions.• In informal English, people often use a plural verb after none of: None of us care what happens to him.USAGE: None, neither•You use none to talk about a group of three or more things or people: None of my friends came.•You use neither to talk about two things or people: Neither of my parents wanted me to go.
