From Longman Dictionary of Contemporary EnglishConsequencesCon·se·quen·ces /ˈkɒnsɪkwənsɪz $ ˈkɑːnsəkwen-/    a game played by a small group of people, in which a story is written with each person adding a sentence without knowing what has already been written. The last sentence begins: ‘The consequence (=result) was...’ The stories are usually about famous people or friends, in amusing or embarrassing situations.
