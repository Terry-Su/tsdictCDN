From Longman Dictionary of Contemporary Englishgossipgos‧sip1 /ˈɡɒsɪp $ ˈɡɑː-/ ●●○ noun    1 [uncountable]RUMOUR/RUMOR information that is passed from one person to another about other people’s behaviour and private lives, often including unkind or untrue remarksgossip about  Here’s an interesting piece of gossip about Mrs Smith.  What’s the latest gossip?
