You say: I like music.We use computers.• Don’t use the with the name of a language. You say: Do you speak English?• Don’t use the with school, prison, college, university, or church when you are talking about them in a general way. You say: Children start school at 4 or 5. She spent a year in prison.• You don’t use the with times and months, or normally with days. You say: at midnighton Tuesdayin May• You don’t normally use the with meals.
