From Longman Dictionary of Contemporary EnglishGreeceGreece /ɡriːs/    a country in southeast Europe on the Mediterranean Sea, which is a member of the EU. Population: 10,772,967 (2014). Capital: Athens. Ancient Greek political ideas, philosophy, art, architecture, literature, and science have had a great influence on how people in Europe live and think. Greece, and especially the many Greek islands, attract large numbers of tourists.
