GRAMMAR: Reciprocal verbsEmbrace is a reciprocal verb. This type of verb is used when saying that two or more people or things do something that involves both or all of them. It does not need to have an object: They embraced. In this sentence, embrace is intransitive and does not have an object.You can also say: He embraced her.They embraced each other.
